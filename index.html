A Thousand Tales

PRERELEASING AUGUST 2023

In a prosperous land churning with the dreams and ambitions of those that have yet to live,


a star shines brightly. Amongst the stars in the starry night sky, it shines brighter than most.


In its constraints it struggles not, and instead strives to dislodge itself from the remains of a bygone past.


When it finally unleashes its capabilities, only the unstoppable marching of time will remain an obstacle in its journey.
